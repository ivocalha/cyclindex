Scoring Logic

UCI Points System

Base reference: official UCI individual ranking points as foundation for race weighting and comparability.

Performance Level Ladder

Custom 7-tier race classification (Grand Tours to .2 events) with dynamic multipliers and prestige coefficients.

Rider Score Formula

Final Cyclindex Rider Score = (UCI Points × Level Multiplier × Form Factor × Consistency Bonus) with 52-week rolling decay.

Team & Nation Score Formula

Aggregated score of top 8 riders (Team) / all registered riders (Nation) with depth and dominance bonuses.

Database Model

Complete technical breakdown of entities, relationships, data flow, sources (ProCyclingStats, FirstCycling, UCI), automated nightly updates, historical integrity, and version control.

Specs Definition

Full transparency: included/excluded races, nationality rules, team transfers policy, minimum race requirements, update schedule, error correction protocol, and open methodology commitment.
