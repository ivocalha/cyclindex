Scoring Logic

UCI Points System

Riders score points depending on their results according to current UCI points matrix. Most valuable result (winning Tour de France) assigns 1300 UCI points, second most valuable (either winning Giro d'Italia or Vuelta a España) assigns 1100 UCI points and so on...

Performance Level Ladder

UCI result points are normalized to a Levels ladder where most valuable possible result equates to a 100 level, second to a 99 level, and so on...

Rider Score Formula

For each rider's score calculation, only the 3 highest scored levels are considered, according to the following formula:

=[(best Level/2+40) × 1 + (2ndbest Level/2+40) × 0,67 + (3rdbest Level/2+40) × 0,33] × 100 / [(100/2+40) × 1 + (99/2+40) × 0,67 + (99/2+40) × 0,33].

Maximum possible score is therefore 100 (for who is able to win the Tour, Giro and Vuelta in the same season) and minimum score is 40.

Team & Nation Score

Best three riders are considered following a simple average calculation between their rider scores.
